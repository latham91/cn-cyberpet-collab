Life on Mars

Welcome to "Life on Mars," a captivating gaming experience set in the year 3024. Immerse yourself in the Martian perspective as you embark on a unique tamagotchi-style journey where humans take center stage. Your mission? To ensure the survival and well-being of these digital humans by managing crucial stats in this futuristic adventure.

In this advanced Martian society, you'll face the challenges of caring for virtual humans with needs as diverse as Health, Oxygen, Hunger, Thirst, energy, and Boredom. Navigate through Earth's historical events, making strategic decisions to maintain the delicate balance of these essential stats.

Keep a close eye on their Health to ensure they stay robust and resilient. Manage their Oxygen levels for survival on Earth. Satisfy their Hunger and Thirst to keep them nourished and hydrated. Combat Fatigue and maintain their energy levels and mental acuity. Additionally, ward off Boredom to support their overall well-being. As you progress, explore the consequences of your choices, shaping the destiny of these virtual humans against the backdrop of Earth's past.

Can you master the intricacies of "Life on Mars" and lead your digital humans through the challenges of history? The fate of these characters rests in your hands as you embark on a unique and captivating journey through time and space.
